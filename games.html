What Remains of Edith Finch

What Remains of Edith Finch is a gripping visual novel, set in a towering house on the remote Orcas island on the coast of Washington. The player acts as the only surviving member of a cursed family, exploring her childhood home, and the stories of the people that once lived there. Following the plot, tragedy after tragedy, you unveil your own doomed fate.

Thimbleweed Park

Thimbleweed park is a fantastic point-and-click mystery adventure. Playing as five seperate player-characters, shifting through storylines and revealing the backstory behind a nearly abandoned town and the current murder case involved.

Chants of Sennaar

Chants of Sennaar is a language puzzle game, where you as the player wake up on the bottom of a six layer tower, learning the languages of the people on the tower as you ascend, and reconnecting the societies as you go. Six seperate writing systems, every one more complex than the last, but equally as fun.

Call of the Sea

Call of the Sea is a visual novel-puzzle game, set on a fictional island in Oceania during the mid-1930s. Playing as a woman set out to find her husband somewhere on the island, and eventually finding the source of her own fantastical genetic condition.

The Secret of Monkey Island

The Secret of Monkey Island is a classic point-and-click adventure game set in the Caribbean, playing as a wanna-be pirate, fulfilling trials and prophecies to save the mayor of Mêlée Island from ghost-pirate LeChuck. A wild ride of puzzles and dialogue, the Monkey Island series is a gem worthy of all praise.

A Basic History On Stained Glass

Stained glass is most typically known in two categories, Tiffany lamps and historic church windows. Though these are the most prevalent forms, this colored glass art also exists as sculpture and jewelry. The first known forms of stained glass existed in ancient times as moulded vases and alike vessels, but had not yet become the cut and leaded art it is today.

Tiffany lamps are often seen as simply beautiful, but an extremely overlooked aspect is the incredibly complex geometric math required to form domes out of flat pieces of glass. Louis Comfort Tiffany was revolutionary in the progression of the art form.
